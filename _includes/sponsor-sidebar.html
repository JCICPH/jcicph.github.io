Become a community sponsor


As a community sponsor you receive a promotion code that you distribute to your colleagues. It's valid for 5 seats at both the conference and the code camp. You get your company logo on the web site and the conference token - and you get to be in the sponsor lineup and give a 3-5 min pitch to the audience at the conference.
